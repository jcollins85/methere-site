Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Summary

MetHere is designed to be local-first. Most of your data stays on your device unless you choose to export or back it up. We collect limited analytics data to help improve the app.

Data You Create in the App

MetHere stores the following data on your device:

  • Venues and places you save
  • People you add and associate with venues
  • Notes and metadata you enter
  • Favourites and pins

This data remains on your device unless you choose to export it or back it up.

Analytics

MetHere uses Firebase Analytics to understand how features are used and to help improve reliability and functionality.

Analytics data may include:

  • Feature interactions (for example, adding, updating, or favouriting a venue)
  • Aggregate usage information and counts
  • The names of venues or places associated with certain feature interactions

Analytics data does not include:

  • People names
  • Notes or descriptions
  • Tags
  • Addresses
  • Exact location coordinates
  • Search query text

Analytics data is associated with a device or app instance and is used only to improve MetHere. MetHere does not sell analytics data.

Location

If you enable proximity features, MetHere may request location permission to notify you when you are near saved venues. Location data is used only for this purpose.

MetHere does not store or transmit precise location coordinates for analytics.

Notifications

If enabled, MetHere uses notifications to deliver reminders related to your saved venues and interactions.

Export and Backup

If you use export or backup features, MetHere creates files (such as CSV or backup files) that you choose where to save, including to the Files app or iCloud Drive.

Third-Party Services

MetHere uses Firebase Analytics. No other third-party analytics or advertising services are used.
