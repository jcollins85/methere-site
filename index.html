How MetHere works

A practical flow from first meeting to timely recall.

Step 1

Capture who you met

Add a person with notes, tags, date met, and role details while everything is still fresh.

Step 2

Link the connection to a place

Attach a venue, pin location context, and control nearby alerts for the places that matter.

Step 3

See everyone met at each venue

Open one place and instantly review all related people, details, and tags in one list.

Step 4

Get reminded at the right moment

Nearby reminders help surface relevant people when you return to saved venues.
