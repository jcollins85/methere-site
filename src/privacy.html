Privacy Policy

Your data stays on your device.

Effective date April 29, 2026

Contact: support@methere.app

Overview

MetHere is a local-first iOS app. The people, places, tags, and notes you add are stored on your device. We do not operate a backend for your content. Some data is sent to third-party services for analytics, crash reporting, and purchases, and to Apple system services to provide core functionality (maps, iCloud, location, notifications, microphone, speech recognition, camera, Photos, and StoreKit).

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require accounts
  • We do not access your contacts or call logs
  • Photos you choose to take or attach stay on your device with your other app data
  • We do not sell your data and do not use it for advertising
  • We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies
  • We do not display ads in MetHere

Information We Collect and Store

Information you enter (stored on your device)

  • People you add (name, position, notes, tags, favorite/heart status, photos, dates, and the encounters you've recorded)
  • Places you add (name, nickname, address/label, coordinates, favorites, proximity alert settings, and counts/timestamps for places you've returned to)
  • Tags and related metadata
  • Moments and milestones earned through app usage
  • Notification history inside the app

Location data

  • Precise location when you capture a place pin or use place search (if you grant permission)
  • Background location if you enable nearby place alerts (geofencing — set to Always)
  • Place coordinates and optional timestamps are stored on your device

MetHere does not transmit your precise location coordinates for advertising or cross-app tracking.

Audio / Speech (optional dictation)

If you use dictation, iOS microphone and speech recognition are used to transcribe your speech into text. MetHere does not store audio recordings. Apple speech services may process audio to provide transcription (some recognition runs on-device on newer iPhones; some is processed by Apple's servers).

Camera and Photos (optional)

  • If you take or pick a photo for a person, the image is resized and saved on your device only — not transmitted to our servers
  • If you save a share card, the image is saved to your Photos library (if you grant permission)
  • If you export or auto-backup, a backup file is written to your device and/or your iCloud Drive

Usage analytics (TelemetryDeck)

MetHere uses TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to understand feature usage and improve reliability. We log app usage events covering navigation, feature usage, errors, onboarding, purchases, proximity alerts, and similar interactions.

  • Event parameters are limited to internal UUIDs (such as a venue ID), enum values (such as a venue type), counts, booleans, durations, and timing buckets
  • We do not send the names, notes, tags, or addresses you enter to analytics. This is enforced by an automated check that runs on every code change and blocks any commit that would violate this rule
  • TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, IDFA, or device fingerprinting. Each device is represented by an anonymous hashed identifier that cannot be linked back to you
  • Analytics may include coarse, non-identifying user properties such as your free/Pro tier, install week, and bucketed counts of people and places, used for cohort analysis

We do not display ads in MetHere and do not use analytics data for advertising. TelemetryDeck processes this data on EU-based servers under its own privacy policy.

Website analytics (Google Analytics)

The MetHere marketing website at methere.app uses Google Analytics 4 to measure page views, section views, navigation usage, App Store/support link clicks, and your privacy choice.

  • This helps us understand website performance and improve site content and conversion paths
  • GA4 may use first-party cookies or similar browser storage to measure repeat visits and engagement
  • We ask for your consent before enabling website analytics — until you accept, no analytics events are sent
  • We configure GA4 with advertising personalization disabled and Google Signals disabled
  • Website analytics does not include the names, notes, tags, or place content entered inside the app

Google may process analytics data according to its own privacy policies. You can change your choice at any time using the Privacy choices button on the site, and you can also reduce tracking through browser controls or Google's Analytics opt-out tools.

Crash reporting (Sentry)

MetHere uses Sentry to monitor app stability and diagnose crashes. If the app crashes or hits an unhandled error, diagnostic information may be sent to Sentry, including device model, operating system version, app version, a stack trace of the failure, and breadcrumbs of recent UI interactions and network requests leading up to the error.

  • Crash reports help us fix bugs and improve reliability
  • We do not send the names, notes, tags, or addresses you enter to Sentry. Breadcrumbs capture the type of action (such as "venue added" or "paywall opened"), not the text you typed
  • Crash data may be associated with a device or app instance identifier

Crash data is processed by Sentry on US-based servers according to their privacy policy and is used solely to improve the stability and performance of MetHere.

Purchases and Subscriptions (RevenueCat + Apple)

MetHere offers Pro access via monthly subscription ($3.99/mo), annual subscription ($14.99/yr), or a one-time lifetime purchase ($29.99). Subscriptions include a 14-day free trial and auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period via your Apple ID Account Settings.

Purchases are processed by Apple and validated with RevenueCat. We receive entitlement and subscription status information (for example: Pro/free status, plan type, trial status, and expiration date). We do not receive your full payment details. RevenueCat and Apple may process device identifiers, transaction identifiers, and subscription lifecycle events to validate purchases, manage renewals, and restore access.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core app features (people, places, tags, insights, share cards)
  • Enable location-based alerts you choose to turn on
  • Enable optional backups, exports, and share cards
  • Understand app usage and improve features (analytics)
  • Monitor stability and fix bugs (crash reporting)
  • Process and validate in-app purchases and restore access when requested

Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data as needed to provide the app:

  • TelemetryDeck for privacy-first app usage analytics
  • Google Analytics (Google) for website analytics on methere.app
  • Sentry for crash reporting and unhandled error diagnostics
  • Firebase Firestore (Google) for short-lived share-link delivery (only when you choose to share contacts with someone)
  • RevenueCat and Apple for purchases and Pro access validation
  • Apple MapKit for place search and map snapshots
  • Apple iCloud Drive for backups you choose to export or auto-backup
  • Apple StoreKit for in-app review prompts
  • Apple system services for location, notifications, microphone, speech recognition, camera, and Photos

If you export a backup or share an image, you control where it goes and who receives it.

Data Storage and Retention

  • Your people and place data is stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud Backup, copies are also written to your iCloud Drive
  • You can reset the app from Settings to delete all local data on this device
  • iCloud Drive copies persist after you uninstall MetHere — you can delete them manually from the Files app or iCloud.com
  • Analytics data is retained by TelemetryDeck according to its privacy policy. Crash reporting data is retained by Sentry according to its privacy policy

Your Choices and Controls

  • You can disable permissions (location, notifications, microphone, camera, photos) at any time in iOS Settings
  • You can reset the app to remove all local data on this device
  • You can delete backups from Files / iCloud Drive at any time
  • You can change your website analytics choice at any time using the Privacy choices button on the site
  • You can request help with deletion of analytics or crash data associated with your device by contacting us

Legal Basis for Processing

We process information based on:

  • Your consent (location, notifications, microphone, camera, Photos permissions; website analytics)
  • Contractual necessity (processing and validating purchases and restore requests)
  • Legitimate interests (understanding feature usage and improving reliability through analytics and crash reporting)

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in the United States, the European Union, or other jurisdictions where our service providers operate (for example, Apple and RevenueCat in the United States, Sentry in the United States, TelemetryDeck in the European Union, Google/Firebase for share-link delivery in the United States). These jurisdictions may have different data protection laws than your region.

Children's Privacy

MetHere is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
