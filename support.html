Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I back up my data?

    In Settings, use Back up data to create a JSON backup file (Pro). You can save it to Files or iCloud Drive.

  • How do I restore my data?

    In Settings, choose Restore backup (Pro), then select a previously exported JSON backup file.

  • What is the difference between JSON backup and CSV export?

    JSON backup is a full snapshot (people, places, tags, and favourites) that you can restore in MetHere. CSV export is a people list for spreadsheets and sharing, and it is not a full in-app restore format. Both are available with Pro.

  • How do I restore purchases?

    Open Settings in MetHere and use Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for purchase.

  • Do I need to manage anything after buying Pro?

    No. Pro is a one-time purchase, not a subscription. If you reinstall the app or move to a new device, use Restore Purchases in Settings to recover access.

  • What are the Free vs Pro limits?

    Free includes up to 15 people, 8 places, and 3 nearby alerts. Pro removes those limits and unlocks backup/export tools.

  • Nearby reminders are not triggering. What should I check?

    Nearby reminders should work even when you are not actively using MetHere. If they are not triggering, check these first:

    • Location Access is set to Always for MetHere.
    • Precise Location is turned On.
    • Notifications are enabled for MetHere.
    • Background App Refresh is enabled.
    • Nearby alerts are turned on for the places you want.
    • Low Power Mode is off (it can limit background activity).
    • You have a stable GPS/cell signal (poor coverage can delay triggers).

    Important: MetHere does not need to be on screen, but if you force-close the app from the app switcher, nearby reminders may stop until you open it again.

  • How do I get a person's name to appear in a nearby alert notification?

    Nearby alerts are tied to places. To include a person's name in a nearby alert:

    • Open the place where that person is listed.
    • Tap the heart next to that person inside the place.
    • Make sure Nearby alerts is turned on for that place.

    When you return to that place, nearby alerts can include people you have hearted in that place.

  • How do I edit or remove a saved place?

    To edit a place, open a person linked to that place, update the place/location details, and save. To remove a place, move or delete the people linked to it. When no people are linked, MetHere removes that place automatically.

  • How do I move a person to a different place?

    Open that person, edit their place, choose the new place, then save.

  • How do I reset app data?

    Use the reset option in Settings to clear local data. Export a backup first if you may need to recover data.
